Akletna App – FAQs

1. General Questions

Q1: What is Akletna?

Akletna is a food delivery platform that connects customers with home chefs and food providers. Through Akletna, you can order homemade meals for daily needs or request catering services for events and gatherings.

Q2: Does Akletna prepare or deliver the food?

No. Akletna is only a platform. The partner (kitchen/catering) is fully responsible for preparing and delivering the food.

Q3: Where is Akletna available?

Akletna is currently available in Egypt, and we are expanding to new areas.

Q4: Is Akletna only for homemade food?

No. Akletna offers two services:

  • Homemade meals: Fresh food for daily orders
  • Catering: Large orders or custom menus for special occasions

2. Account & Registration

Q5: How do I create an account?

Download the Akletna App, register with your name, email, and phone number, and complete verification.

Q6: Do I need to pay to register?

No. Registration is totally free.

Q7: Can I browse without an account?

Yes, there is a guest mode option where you can browse, but you need an account to place an order and access more features.

3. Ordering & Payments

Q8: How do I place a homemade meal order?

  • Open the app
  • Select a kitchen
  • Choose your meals
  • Add to cart
  • Confirm delivery details and pay

Q9: How do I place a catering order?

  • Open the Catering section
  • Select a catering
  • Choose from available packages
  • Set event name, delivery date, time, location, and complete the details
  • Request and wait for the confirmation from the catering to pay

Q10: Do I need to order catering in advance?

Yes. Catering orders must be placed ahead of time. Each partner specifies their preparation and notice period.

Q11: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards and cash on delivery.

Q12: Can I cancel or edit my order?

  • Homemade meals: Can be canceled before preparation starts.
  • Catering orders: Can be canceled before preparation starts.

Q13: Do I get a refund if I cancel?

Refunds depend on the status of the order and partner policy. If canceled early and before preparation starts, you may receive a full refund.

4. Delivery

Q14: Who delivers the food?

Delivery is the responsibility of the partner (kitchen/catering). Akletna does not handle delivery directly.

Q15: Can I track my order?

Yes, you can check your order status updates.

Q16: What if my order is late, missing, or incorrect?

Navigate to the profile section and tap on get help where you can report it to Akletna support team for assistance.

5. Support & Contact不满 Contacting

Q17: How do I contact support?

You can use the in-app support get help or email Support@akletna.app

6. Safety & Responsibility

Q19: Who is responsible for food quality and hygiene?

The partner (kitchen/catering) is fully responsible for preparing safe, hygienic food. Akletna only provides the platform to connect you with them.

Q20: What if I have a food safety concern?

Report the issue via the app or to support. We will take action against partners who violate food safety standards.

Disclaimer: Akletna does not cook, prepare, or deliver food. Responsibility for preparation, packaging, and delivery lies solely with the partner.

Akletna Partner App – FAQs

1. General Questions

Q1: What is Akletna Partner App?

The Akletna Partner App allows home chefs and food providers to sell homemade meals and offer catering services. It helps partners manage menus, receive orders, and track earnings.

Q2: Does Akletna prepare or deliver the food?

No. Akletna is only a platform. As a partner, you are responsible for preparing, packaging, and delivering the food to customers.

Q3: Who can join as a partner?

Any verified home chef, kitchen, or catering provider who meets Akletna’s requirements.

2. Registration & Setup

Q4: How do I register as a partner and what to do if I forgot my password?

  • Download the Akletna Partner App
  • Sign up and provide your details
  • Wait for approval and our team to contact (usually 1–3 business days)
In case of forgetting your password, on the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the reset steps and verification or you can log in using your phone number.

Q5: Is there a registration fee?

No, partners can register for free and start using the app.

3. Menu & Catering Management

Q6: How do I add homemade meals to my menu?

Go to Menu → Add section then add a meal → Upload photo, description, price and details → Save.

Q7: How do I offer catering services?

  • Open the Catering section
  • Create catering menus/packages
  • Set your catering details
  • Publish your offers for customers to see

Q8: Can I set a minimum order for catering?

Yes. You can handle all your profile settings and menus as you want.

Q9: How do I receive orders?

You will get instant notifications in the Partner App with customer details and order requirements.

4. Payments & Earnings

Q10: How do I get paid?

You can provide your IBAN Number in the profile section and earnings are transferred to your bank account.

Q11: How often are payouts processed?

Weekly or bi-weekly, based on your agreement.

Q12: Can I request deposits for catering orders?

Yes, you can control your deposit percentage and customers must pay a deposit or the total amount in advance in order to confirm.

Q13: Can I track my earnings?

Yes. The app includes an Earnings Dashboard for sales, total orders, and payout history.

5. Delivery

Q14: Who delivers the food?

You, the partner, are fully responsible for delivering both homemade meals and catering orders. Akletna does not deliver food.

Q15: What about packaging?

Partners must ensure food is safely and properly packaged before delivery.

6. Customer Relations

Q16: Can customers cancel orders?

Yes. If canceled before you start preparing, no issue. If canceled after preparation has started, you may still receive partial compensation depending on policy.

7. Support & Growth

Q19: How do I contact support?

By emailing Support@akletna.app and our team will contact.

Q20: Does Akletna promote my food?

Yes. We may feature partners in seasonal promotions and social media campaigns.

Q21: Can I offer discounts or promotions?

Yes. You can create special offers and discounts from the Partner App.

8. Responsibility Disclaimer

Q22: Who is responsible for food preparation and delivery?

You, the partner, are solely responsible for:

  • Preparing the food
  • Ensuring hygiene and safety
  • Packaging properly
  • Delivering the order

Disclaimer: Akletna only provides the technology platform to connect you with customers. Akletna does not cook, prepare, or deliver food.
